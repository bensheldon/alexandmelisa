Where to Stay

Hotels in the Marina District of San Francisco

The Marina District is a fun neighborhood located near our wedding venue. There is tons of shopping, eating and drinking to be done in that part of town and you're right by the Golden Gate Bridge and the beautiful bay with all it's sail boats.

The Coventry Inn
Nice big rooms in a great historic hotel. Parking available for 1 car per room.

The Marina Motel
Quirky little historic San Francisco motel. This place is pet friendly and some of the rooms have kitchens, which can be great if you are staying more than a couple nights (although the food is fantastic in this part of town). Parking is included for 1 car per room and they give you buy 1 get 1 free breakfasts at one of the local cafes.
